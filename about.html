Why did we create saturdaycoach.com?

This project came together through one of our master's level classes. Our professor asked us to create a data driven application with at least 10 web forms. Originally, we were not planning on working together, but as our teacher was going around the class talking to students about their project proposals, we figured out that we both wanted to do something with college football. For the project, Neville focused on gaining insight into 2 point conversions and when they were most successful. He also looked at what teams were most successful with two point conversions. Griffin, on the other hand, worked to present data in ways different than how it is presented on ESPN and Yahoo, which led to more valuable insights into college football. We both gained substantial knowledge about college football by creating saturdaycoach.com and we want to share it with you!
