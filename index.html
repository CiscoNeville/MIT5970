Seven minutes left... Your team just scored a touchdown to go up by 5... Should the coach go for 2 or play it safe with the PAT?

Think you know the answer? How sure are you of that answer? Based on what, a gut feel or something more concrete?

Now you can find the correct answer to this question and more using big data! This site links to a database full of 15 years of play-by-play data (data courtsey of BlueSCar) and you can query all that data for the insights you want.

Use the queries drop down (just above) to get started!
